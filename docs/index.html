I am a PhD candidate at School of Computing and Engineering in University of Huddersfield, UK. I am also a member of Centre for Visual and Immersive Computing (CVIC). My research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Edge Computing, High Performance Computing (HPC) and GPU Computing.
